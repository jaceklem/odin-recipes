Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, etc.

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  6. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired pizza crust thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese. Add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
